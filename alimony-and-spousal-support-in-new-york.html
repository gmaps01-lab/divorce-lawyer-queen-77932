Frequently Asked Questions About Alimony and Spousal Support

How is alimony or spousal support determined in Queens, NY, and what factors will the court consider?
In Queens and throughout New York, alimony (also known as spousal maintenance) is determined based on a set of statutory guidelines, but the court also considers factors such as the length of the marriage, each spouse’s income and earning potential, age and health, contributions to the household, and the standard of living established during the marriage. At Aronov Family Law NY, P.C., we’ll assess your unique situation and help you understand how these factors could impact your case.
What is the typical process for filing for spousal support with your firm, and how long does it usually take?
The process starts with an initial consultation, where we review your financial situation and goals. If you decide to move forward, we gather the necessary documentation and file the appropriate petitions with the court. The timeline varies, but temporary spousal support can sometimes be awarded within a few weeks if urgent, while a final support order can take several months depending on court schedules and whether the case is settled or litigated. We keep you informed at every step and work to resolve your case efficiently.
